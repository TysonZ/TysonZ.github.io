These changes allowed me to scale back my presence to a simple sub 1MB website. If this interested you, please feel free to read a little more about the process here.

1️⃣ Significance of ¹

To maintain the small footprint of this website, I occasionally embed optional content from external sources. Whenever you encounter the ¹ symbol, it indicates that the page includes embedded external content.

tyson@Tysons-MacBook-Air tysonznet % du -sh public
384K	public
By employing this strategy, I can keep the site lightweight while still incorporating relevant images and videos through embedding non-critical content from third-party sources.
