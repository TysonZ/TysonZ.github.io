Dragos ICS/OT Cybersecurity Course

Overview

I was invited to attend Dragos Academy’s ICS/OT Cyber Security Training, a virtual instructor lead training (VILT) course delivered by ICS Security expert Mark Heard. The course spanned over two days and both introduced students to ICS Networks, and educated students on how to safely assess an ICS environment from a Cybersecurity perspective.

Review

I greatly enjoyed my two days with Mark learning about Industrial Control Systems, and even more so with the interactive labs. The course was designed to accommodate both Cybersecurity professionals who don’t have much exposure to ICSs, and ICS professionals without experience in Cybersecurity.

Benefits
  • Enhanced understanding of OT systems
  • Learnt cybersecurity concepts tailored to ICS and OT environments
  • Safe assessment of ICS Networks
  • Hands on penetration testing labs
  • Certificate of Completion which count towards CLE hours
